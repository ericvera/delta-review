Delta Review — Reviewed bucket in the cluster view

Grouped (cluster) mode gains a Reviewed bucket at the bottom of the view. Marking a file reviewed moves its row out of its cluster into Reviewed (unmarking or editing it moves it back — everything stays derived from content). Clusters keep their reviewed/total counts, so per-cluster progress is still visible. The ungrouped view is unchanged. Rows with visible +/−/open icons simulate mouse hover.
